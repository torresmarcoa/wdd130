Overview

Purpose

This webpage's purpose is to offer the best white water rafting services online with a user-friendly, accessible, and luring webpage that will increase its popularity nationally and internationally. And generate a great flow of visits and new clients that will increase the company's revenue and presence online.

Audience

The target audience for this website is working young adults and adults, both single and married, around 18 to 30, with a passion for adventure and new experiences, and also for those experienced in white water rafting that seeks to practice their ability in the most beautiful rivers nature can offer. As stated earlier, they have a passion for adventure that cannot be met in their normal day-to-day activities. So we offer them the best opportunity for adventure by practicing white water rafting. Most of them will usually access the website on their laptops and cellphones as they seek activities full of emotion and adventure.
